About Me

Hi, I'm Gurvansh Singh Juneja — a photographer, visual storyteller, and creative soul who finds beauty in the unspoken moments. I believe that my photography isn't about perfection; but about presence.

Over the years, I've curated hundreds of visuals that reflect not just the world around me, but how I feel about it — from the warmth of sleepy street dogs to the serenity of forgotten temples, from playful kittens to textured shadows.

I'm currently pursuing a degree in tech but my heart beats for design, storytelling, and human connection — whether that's through photography, building immersive websites, or just sitting under a sky that's pink for no reason.

This portfolio is a slice of how I see the world. I hope you see a bit of yourself in it too.
